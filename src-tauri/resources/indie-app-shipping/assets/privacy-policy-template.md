# Privacy Policy — Template

Fill the `[bracketed]` placeholders. This is a practical starting template, **not legal advice** — adapt it to the data your app actually collects and to the laws that apply to your users (e.g. Vietnam's Decree 13/2023 on personal data, GDPR for EU users, Apple privacy labels / Google Play Data safety must match this document). The disclosures here must agree with your store privacy declarations and your app's real behavior, or you risk store enforcement.

Host this at a public URL (e.g. on your own domain) and link it in the app, in the store listing, and — for the deletion section — in the Data safety form / App Privacy.
