# Terms of Use / EULA — Template

**Not legal advice** — a practical starting template; adapt to your jurisdiction. Required (linked in-app and on the paywall) when the app has a **subscription**. Both stores accept their standard licensed-application EULA:

- **Apple:** if you don't supply your own EULA, Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement applies by default. You only need a custom EULA if you have specific terms.
- **Google Play:** link your own Terms; there's no single mandated EULA, but subscription terms must be clear.

Use the standard EULA unless you have a real reason to write your own. If you do need custom terms, the skeleton below covers the essentials. The **subscription disclosure block** at the bottom is the part stores actually check — include it on the paywall and in the store description for auto-renewing subscriptions.
